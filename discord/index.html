The rules are simple. Before getting any further, make sure to comply with the rules given below. You agree with Discord's TOS to proceed to this server, any violation of it will be bannable by Discord.
1. No toxic behavior or minimodding!
Please act maturely on discussions. Don't rage or spam. If you feel the drama is unavoidable, either move it somewhere else or do not escalate it and notify any staff member about it. Don't moderate the server, it's the work of staff team.
2. Make all your discussions in English.
Though foreign languages aren't harshly forbidden. Derails caused by usage of any language other than English can result in a mute.
3. Avoid racial slurs or personal attacks.
You have to understand that all of us are different. Any attack related to someone will result on a strike & mute or ban depending at the situation.
4. Do not post any kind NSFW content on the server.
5. Use common sense.
6. Do not ask for a promotion.
Any role will be given respectfully depending on your activity. Be nice!
